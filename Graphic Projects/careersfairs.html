Careers Fairs

Careers Fairs Promotional Materials

This page showcases my graphic design contributions to the university's Careers Fairs, focusing on creating essential promotional and informational materials. These pieces were crucial in engaging both students and over 190 attending employers, ensuring a smooth and informative experience at these large-scale events. My work involved developing new designs and iteratively refining them to meet dynamic event needs.

Employer Welcome Postcard

I designed this postcard as a key component of the exhibitor kit provided to over 180 employers attending the two main Careers Fairs. This postcard served as a greeting and initial point of contact, reinforcing the appreciation for their participation.

CV Flyer

This flyer was included in the exhibitor kit for attending employers, specifically designed to advertise the Careers Service's new digital CV feature. The feature allows employers to conveniently access student CVs online after the fair. My design aimed to clearly and concisely communicate the benefits and ease of use of this innovative digital tool, encouraging employer engagement and streamlining post-fair recruitment efforts.

Autumn Careers Fair Floorplan

This floor plan was designed for the University of Limerick's main Autumn Careers Fair in 2024, a large-scale event encompassing a wide array of industries and over 160 employers. Its detailed grid showcases numerous individual employer booths, each numbered for easy navigation. This comprehensive design was continuously refined and adjusted from the initial booking period right up to the morning of the fair to ensure seamless flow and accessibility for thousands of students and diverse recruiting organisations.

UL Law Careers Fair Floorplan

This floor plan was designed for the Law Careers Fair in 2024 and details the event's layout. Designed for a more specialised and intimate fair, the plan clearly outlines the "Exhibitor Lunch Area" and the "Student Entrance." Despite its smaller scale compared to the general careers fair, this floor plan also underwent iterative adjustments during the planning stages to optimise the experience for both legal professionals and students seeking careers in law.
