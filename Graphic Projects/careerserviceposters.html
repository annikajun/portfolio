Careers Service



Careers Service Posters

This page showcases a comprehensive collection of posters designed for the university's Careers Service. My work involved creating promotional materials for various initiatives, including Careers Fairs, workshops, and specialised services like CV Clinics. A key focus was on ensuring brand coherence across all designs, making the Careers Service's communications instantly recognisable to students and faculty.



Newly Designed Promotional Posters

This carousel features a selection of newly designed posters that I created from scratch. These materials were developed to promote a variety of Careers Service initiatives, including general service awareness, Career Service programmes, upcoming workshops, and key events like the annual Career Fairs. The aim was to capture student attention and clearly convey the benefits of engaging with the Careers Service.



Brand Revitalisation

This section displays posters that underwent a significant brand refresh and coherence update. While the original content existed, I redesigned these posters to align with updated branding guidelines and ensure a consistent visual identity across all Careers Service communications. This effort helped create a unified and professional look, making the posters instantly recognisable as official Careers Service materials.



Graduate Outcomes Survey (GOS) Campaigns

This carousel highlights posters specifically designed for the Graduate Outcomes Survey (GOS). These posters were distributed across various university faculties to showcase the results, which showcased information on graduates' employment and further study paths nine months after graduation.



Careersmas Initiative

These posters were designed for "Careersmas," a unique 12-day initiative hosted by the Careers Service in December. My work involved updating the branding and promoting a new prize challenge associated with the initiative. Careersmas aimed to engage students with daily career tips through LinkedIn newsletter articles, each containing a hidden code for entry into a prize draw.
