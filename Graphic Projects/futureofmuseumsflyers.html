Future of Museums



Future of Museums: Engaging Audiences with Immersive Technologies

This project involved the design of informative flyers for "The Future of Museums," a research initiative exploring the integration of Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR) within museum settings. The goal was to create visually engaging and accessible materials that communicate the potential of these interactive technologies to enhance the museum experience.



The design effectively distills key insights from qualitative research, including visitor engagement, ethical considerations, and the impact on museum professionals, into a compelling visual narrative. The flyers aim to attract and inform a diverse audience about how museums can remain relevant and captivating in an increasingly digital age by thoughtfully embracing technological innovation.
