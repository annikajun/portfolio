School Placement



School Placement Posters

This page features a collection of posters designed to support the School Placement office in the Education Department at the University of Limerick. My role involved creating clear and informative promotional materials for various aspects of the program, including essential briefing sessions and CV clinics. The goal was to ensure all communications were easily understandable and visually consistent, helping education students navigate their school placements effectively.



Informational and Workshop Posters

This carousel showcases posters designed to inform students about crucial aspects of their school placement journey, including the mandatory Briefing sessions and CV Clinics.



Service Promotion and Engagement

This section features posters created to promote the overall School Placement Service and encourage students to optimise their placement experience. The designs focus on highlighting the benefits and resources available, aiming to maximise student engagement and success during their placements.
